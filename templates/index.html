What is a MIDI Sampler?

A MIDI sampler is a type of digital musical instrument that uses pre-recorded sounds or "samples" to create music. These samples are typically stored in files called soundfonts.

Soundfonts are a popular format for distributing collections of audio samples. They can be used to recreate the sounds of real instruments or create entirely new sounds.

With a MIDI sampler, musicians can load different soundfonts to access a wide variety of sounds and play them using MIDI controllers, such as keyboards or drum pads.

What is PiJama Sampler?

A MIDI sampler that can add more sounds to your midi device and runs over the Raspberrypi. It has a web UI and it's designed for live performances and easy sampler aditions and device adiministration.
